The King of Fighters XV is the latest installment in the KOF series. Announced at EVO 2019, the game was revealed in January 2021. KOF XV was released worldwide on 17 February 2022. The base roster includes 39 characters with 22 DLCs added over the course of several years after:

  • Team Garou was released on 17 March 2022
  • Omega Rugal was released on 14 April 2022
  • Team South Town was released on 16 May 2022
  • Team Awakened Orochi was released on 8 August 2022
  • Team Samurai was released on 4 October 2022
  • Shingo Yabuki was released on 17 January 2023
  • Kim Kaphwan was released on 4 April 2023
  • Sylvie Paula Paula was released on 16 May 2023
  • Goenitz was released on 20 June 2023
  • Najd was released on 8 August 2023
  • Duo Lon was released on 12 September 2023
  • Hinako Shijo was released on 14 November 2023.
  • Vice and Mature were released on 12 December 2024.
